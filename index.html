Mapping Data

  1. Visit 596acres.org and download their spreadsheet
  2. ...or just get the URL to the CSV
  3. Upload the file or register the URL at GeoCommons.com
  4. Walk through the process. You could do this in the map, but we have time...
  5. Choose "Map Data"
  6. Choose to Style by Color or Size
  7. Embed in facebook

Project: Community Projects Analysis

Analyzing your Data

  1. Continuing from the "596 Acres map layer"...
  2. Click on the layer, choose 'Analyze'
  3. Choose "Aggregation"
  4. Change Boundary, Search for "NYC Boundary"
  5. Choose "Brooklyn Neighborhood Boundaries"
  6. Add "Area (sq ft.)" Average and "Area (sq. ft.)" Sum
  7. After it finishes processing, choose to style color by "Sum of area".
  8. Re-order the layers and play with styling.

check out geocommons.com/maps/196311
